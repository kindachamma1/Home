My Bachelor Journey

A tale of learning across two universities.

Part 1: Avignon University

I began my Bachelor studies at Avignon University, where I was introduced to foundational concepts in computer science and mathematics. The environment fostered my curiosity in algorithmic thinking and gave me a solid grounding in programming and system design.

Part 2: Linnaeus University

Later, I had the opportunity to study at Linnaeus University in Sweden. Here, I deepened my understanding of data structures, machine learning basics, and human-centered computing. The international exposure enriched my academic and cultural experience significantly.
